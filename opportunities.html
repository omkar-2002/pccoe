Opportunities

Computer Engineering Students Association (CESA)

CESA is an active student association in the department, which works for the conduction of various activities for overall development of students under the guidance of professors.

  • Student Chapters of National & International professional bodies lead by students
    • PCCOE Association for Computing Machinery Student Chapter (PCCOE ACM Student Chapter)
    • Awarded as “ACM Best Student Chapter Runner UP” for the year 2018 & 2020
    • Awarded with Outstanding community service award for the year 2020 at International Level
    PCCOE Association for Computing Machinery Student Chapter for women (PCCOE ACM-W student Chapter)
    Exclusive student chapter for girls
    Events handled are:
    • Hour of Code
    • ACM - W Regional
    The Institution of Engineering and Technology Student Chapter (IET on Campus)
    Computer Society of India student Chapter (CSI Student Chapter)
    Events handled are:
    • Girlscript Program
    • Antenna design internship
    • Cyber security webinar

  • Various Clubs for technical skill up gradations:
    • Google Developer Students Club (DSC)
    Technical club managed by students which hosts sessions and training camps regarding new emerging technologies.
    • Headstart October
    • Android Study Jam
    • Competitive coding sessions
    • Robotics & IOT workshop
    Google Cloud ready facilitator program:
    As an initiative to learn the fundamentals of Google Cloud, a campaign named ‘30. Days of Google Cloud’ is initiated in PCCoE under the Google Cloud ready facilitator Program.
    CESA Coding Club:
    Coding club is lead by students and guided by professors which works to groom In the coding arena.
    • Organizes weekly coding contests
    • Hackathons under Anantya

  • Other Cells
    • Communication And Personality Development Cell
    Various activities are conducted for the welfare and overall development of students.
    Corporate speaker sessions are arranged to give an insight about the communication in the corporate world.
    SIG
    Students are assigned a guide with expertise in a particular field
    ISR
    To Inculcate Social awareness, values and environmentally responsible behaviour amongst students.
    Photography Cell
    Various events are organized at institute level where students can show their photography skills.
